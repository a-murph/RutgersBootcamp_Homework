Bio

Thank You Scientist is an American progressive rock band from Montclair, New Jersey, United States. Their debut studio album Maps of Non-Existent Places was named the Revolver Magazine "Album of the Week" in October 2014. Their second album Stranger Heads Prevail was released in July 2016.
